Welcome to BLZ

A platform where you can easily find your legislators and keep track of track legislation that is important to you.

Sign Up or Login to access your dashboard and save bills you are interested in!

Bills

Browse bills for the 117th congress. Refine your search by choosing a Policy area. If you need to narrow it down futher you can select a date range to search as well.

Legislators

Browse legislators for the 117th congress. Refine your search by cState, Party, and Position. From there you can select a legislator, and view what bills they have recently sponsored. You can also view how to contact them, connect with them on social media.

Dashboard

View your dashboard, where you can see all the legislators for your state, as well as all the bills you would like to save. Sign up to access your dashboard!
